PomoTimer

25 minutes timer for studying

My Story

As a medical student and developer,I built this app for the days when focus feels impossible and motivation is low. It’s not about doing everything, it’s about doing something, one session at a time.

Pomodoro Timer is gotten from the pomodoro technique by Francesco Cirillo a university student who also struggled with procastination

It is time management method using a timer to break work into focused 25-minute intervals (pomodoros), separated by short 5-minute breaks, with a longer break after four cycles to boost focus, reduce burnout, and combat procrastination by making large tasks less daunting
